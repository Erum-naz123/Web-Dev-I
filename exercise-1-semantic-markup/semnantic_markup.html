Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious homemade cookies with gooey chocolate chips!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

If you don't have eggs, you can use a suitable substitute like applesauce, mashed bananas, or yogurt. Use 1/4 cup of the substitute for each egg.

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 190°C.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. Cream together butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture and mix well.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop tablespoons of dough onto baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 8–10 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Let cookies cool before serving.
